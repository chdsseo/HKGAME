About SIG

Company Milestones

First Stage: Start-up Period (1991-1996)
Mission in this Stage: To become the most well-known game development company in the Chinese region
Skytree Innovations Co., Ltd. was officially established in 1991. While there were immense market opportunities in arcade games, most mid- to high-end products were in the hands of major Japanese game developers. In response to this, with its adherence to its own brand and independent R&D of key technologies, SIG entered the market which Japan had a monopoly on for a long time. It aimed to become the most well-known arcade game development company in the Chinese region. Key games developed in this stage: Fighting game "Dragon World"; puzzle game "Alien Challenge".
  • 1991
    Establishment of Skytree Innovations Co., Ltd.
    SIG business scope covered design, R&D, manufacturing, marketing and after-sales services for arcade game software, hardware, and institutions.
  • 1992
    Launched the first fighting arcade game, "Dragon World".
  • 1993
    Launched the first puzzle arcade game, "Alien Challenge".
  • 1994
    Ranked as one of the most excellent exporters/importers by the Ministry of Economic Affairs for the first time.


Second Stage: Arcade Games Took Off (1996-1999)
Mission in this Stage: To become the largest game development company in the Chinese region
In 1996, the 2D arcade console PolyGame Master (or PGM) was successfully developed. SIG became the only major game developer beside Japanese companies to have its own arcade system board in the Asian region. Using this game platform as a foundation, SIG not only successfully developed a variety of puzzle games, but also established a firm foothold for cross-platform game porting in the future. In this stage, PGM continuously launched excellent games appreciated by the masses, including "Oriental Legend", "The Killing Blade", "Knights of Valour". The company continued to invest in R&D resources. In 1999, it successfully developed the first 3D racing game, taking Japanese companies out of their exclusive position in 3D simulators. SIG' outstanding performance in the market made it become the largest game development company in the Chinese region.
  • 1996
    The 2D arcade console (PolyGame Master or PGM), was successfully developed.
    Launched the first PGM scrolling action game, "Oriental Legend".
  • 1998
    Ranked as one of CommonWealth Magazine's top 1000 manufacturers for the first time (the 943rd).
    Launched the first PGM two-player combat game, "The Killing Blade".
  • 1999
    Ranked as one of CommonWealth Magazine's cross-industry top 2000 companies for the first time (the 1641st; and the 903rd out of top 1000 manufacturers).
    Successfully developed the first 3D racing game, "Speed Driver".
    Launched "Knights of Valour", the classic PGM scrolling action game.


Third Stage: Rapid Development Across Different Platforms (2000-2003)
Mission in this Stage: To become an all-round game development company in the Chinese region
SIG has always adhered to the business principle of "focus on the core; independent R&D". The company continues to strengthen its core game technology as market trends and industry technology evolve. In this stage, SIG not only improved its porting technology across arcade and PC platforms, but also gained momentum from the internet revolution for its next wave of development. In the field of arcade games, the R&D team continuously made breakthroughs when technological bottleneck emerged. They fully utilized different features of PGM, TSG, and Atomiswave to develop and port games. The company also yielded excellent achievements in the simulator market, and became a top brand in the simulator market of the Greater China region.
SIG developed even more rapidly in the PC game field. Within just three years, the company was already able to develop different genres of single-player, local network, and cyber games for PC platform. The company also established its second division - the online game division.
  • 2000
    Business scope began to involve PC game design and R&D.
    Launched the first single-player game for PC "star31".
    Launched the first musical rhythm arcade game "Rock Fever DJ".
    Launched "Knights of Valour 2", the PGM scrolling action game.
  • 2001

  • 2002

    Launched the first game for PC network "star31 2002".
  • 2003

    Business scope began to involve online game design, R&D, marketing, and operation.
    Successfully developed Touch Screen Game (or TSG)
    Launched "Fong Sheng Online", the first MMORPG with tens of thousands of players.
    Launched the first touch screen arcade game "EZ Touch 2".
    Launched the first touch screen arcade game connected to internet "EZ Touch 2 Plus".


Fourth Stage: Overseas Expansion (2004-Present)
Mission in this Stage: To develop toward the capital market and become a world-class game development company
The game industry is technology-intensive, brain-intensive, and cutting-edge. With the aim to strengthen the adherence to its principles - focusing on its own brand and independent R&D - SIG understood deeply that only elevating the enterprise's brand awareness and reputation could attract even more outstanding industry talents. The company also publicly raised funds to move towards becoming global. It proactively received emerging stock market consultation. It applied to be on the emerging stock market on March 12, 2004, and officially got listed on the emerging stock market on July 12, 2006. The company will continue to focus on developing its core business - games - and it has set these specific goals for the future: One: To hold the highest market share in the simulator market globally. Two: To become the best provider of online game content. Three: To form the best game R&D team, and to implement global business strategies while gradually reaching the long-term goal of becoming globalized and world-class.
